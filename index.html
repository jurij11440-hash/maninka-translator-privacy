Privacy Policy — Maninka Translator — Guinea (Voice + Pro)

SAFE This policy explains how the app processes data. The app is designed for privacy and can work offline.

1) What the app does

Maninka Translator — Guinea (Voice + Pro) provides voice translation and text translation to help communication in Guinea (including Banankoro) with support for multiple local dialects.

2) Data collection

We do not collect personal data. The app does not require account login.

3) Microphone usage

The microphone is used only to recognize speech and produce translation. When Screen Off Mode is enabled by the user, the app may run a foreground service with a persistent notification to keep translation stable.

4) Storage

The app may store settings locally on your device (for example: selected language, voice style, and offline packs).

5) Diagnostics (Safe Export)

The app can export safe diagnostics for troubleshooting. Safe Export removes or masks sensitive content (such as speech text, contacts, phone numbers, coordinates, and secrets).

6) Sharing

No data is shared with third parties by default. If you use export/share features, you choose what to share and with whom.

7) Children

The app can be used by a general audience, including children, under adult supervision.

8) Contact

Support email: jurij11440@gmail.com

9) Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect improvements and compliance requirements.
